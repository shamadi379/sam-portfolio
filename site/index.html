About — Portfolio

I fell in love with computers at age seven — pulling apart old PCs, learning how they worked, and solving puzzles for the sheer joy of discovery. That curiosity led me into study and junior IT support roles, where I honed communication, triage, and a customer-first approach that still guides how I work.

Today I’m a System Administrator working across on-prem and Azure environments, focused on reliability and practical automation. I regularly use Azure (Entra ID, ARM, VNets, VMs), Microsoft 365, Intune, Windows Server, Veeam backups, PowerShell and Azure Automation. I prefer simple, repeatable solutions — clean automations, clear runbooks, and measurable monitoring that keep systems resilient.

Outside of work I’m a nature lover who values simplicity — long walks, quiet mornings, and photography help me reset and return to work with clarity. That same taste for simplicity shapes my technical decisions: pragmatic, maintainable, and focused on delivering outcomes people can rely on.
